History

"Founded in 2005 by two adventure enthusiasts, White Water Rafting began with a single raft and a passion for sharing the thrill of the rapids. Starting on the Colorado River, we quickly became known for delivering unforgettable water rafting experiences rooted in safety, personalized care, and client satisfaction. Today, we proudly guide adventurers of all levels through some of the most iconic rivers, staying true to our mission of creating memories that last a lifetime."
